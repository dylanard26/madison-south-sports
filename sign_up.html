Signing Up For The League

Signing up for the league is the most crucial step, but it's also the easiest. All you have to do is come to the Malesus Community Center in Jackson, Tennessee around the 1st to the 18th of March. You must have your child's birth certificate and $25 to pay for fees. There will also be a form for you to fill out regarding your child's medical history or any other special problems we should know about.

Online sign-ups is something we are working towards but have not figured out yet so all we can do is give you information, and a link to the sign-up sheet below.
