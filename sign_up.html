Signing Up For The League

Signing up for the league is the most crucial step, but it's also the easiest. All you have to do is be sure you completely filled out the form and then come to the Malesus Community Center in Jackson, Tennessee around the 1st to the 18th of March. You must have your child's birth certificate and $25 to pay for fees.
