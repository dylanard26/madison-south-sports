Meet the Board Members

image of all board members go here-->

We have 13 total members on the Madison South Sports Board. While you are here, you will get to learn a little more about who they are and what they do.

Shane and Jamie Wimberley

Shane and Jamie are the main head of the board and make most of the main decisions for the park. They were initially put in charge of the park after the old owners gave it up. They handle a lot of the behind-the-scenes work and sign-ups for the league. They have 4 children.

image of shane and jamie-->

Billy and Mary Ard

Billy and Mary are another familiar face of the board, they are almost always up at the park. They handle food and drink ordering. They have two kids and both of them play in the league and Billy coaches his daughter's team.
